What you will learn

  • Understand solar panel types, mounting basics and site inspection.
  • Learn inverter, battery and charge controller connection flow.
  • Practice wiring safety, earthing and load checks.
  • Identify common solar maintenance complaints and service steps.
  • Prepare customer handover and maintenance checklist.
