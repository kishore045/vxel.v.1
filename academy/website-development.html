5 questions for this course
1. Which two main units are used in a split AC?

Answer: Indoor and outdoor unit

2. Before opening an AC panel, what should be done first?

Answer: Switch off power supply

3. Water leakage in split AC is commonly related to what?

Answer: Drain blockage

4. Low cooling complaint should first be checked with?

Answer: Airflow, filter and temperature setting

5. Which tool is commonly used for AC gas pressure checking?

Answer: Manifold gauge
