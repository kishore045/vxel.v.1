What you will learn

  • Identify top-load and front-load washing machine components.
  • Practice inlet valve, drain motor, belt, tub and spin complaint checks.
  • Understand wiring safety and basic board handling precautions.
  • Learn customer-site inspection and demo procedure.

5 questions for this course
1. Washing machine drain complaint is related to?

Answer: Drain motor or pipe

2. Heavy vibration can happen due to?

Answer: Uneven leveling

3. Before checking wiring, technician must?

Answer: Switch off power

4. Inlet valve controls what?

Answer: Water filling

5. Final test should check?

Answer: Wash, drain and spin
